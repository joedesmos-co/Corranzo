Corranzo is a music notation software as well as a playback software and a learning tool. You upload a pdf and you can annotate it. You upload a midi and a mxl / xml file you can learn with a wait for you mode and preview and listen to the piece.

Website: https://corranzo.com

To try my app just click the link and you will be brought to the homepage where you can test a demo song or upload your own!

Features:
* Upload your own pdf and annotate it.
* Upload your own pdf, midi, and mxl/xml and preivew your score and hear it.
* Upload your own pdf, midi, and mxl/xml and use wait for you mode which listens to your piano via mic or midi and waits for you to play a note and then moves on.
* Profile page that shows stats of how long you have practiced.
* Session tracker on profile page that allows you to record your session then when you are done you can fill out info about your session.

Technical stuff:
Corranzo runs entirely in the browser, so your sheet music, MIDI files, annotations, and practice data never leave your device. The hardest part was getting PDFs to line up with MusicXML/MIDI timing, so I built a calibration system that analyzes each score and chooses the most accurate alignment automatically but it took a very long time and was super hard to build.
